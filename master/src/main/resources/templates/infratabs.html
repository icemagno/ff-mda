Data Exchange

Uses peer-to-peer transfer over a synchronous HTTPS transport, backed by Mutual TLS authentication. X509 certificate exchange is orchestrated by FireFly, such that self-signed certificates can be used (or multiple PKI trust roots) and bound to the blockchain-backed identities of the organizations in FireFly.
